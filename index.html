What is betsteem?

betsteem is a betting exchange powered by the Steem BlockChain. Instead of using fiat money you back or lay outcomes of different sport competitions by using your Steem voting power and compete against other users who "vote" on the other side of your selection.

How it works?

You can place back or lay "votes" on any sports event.

HOME 3 vs 1 VISITOR

A back vote means you're betting for that team to win and a lay vote means you're betting for that team not to win, that means to lose or draw.
